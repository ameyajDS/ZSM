Following operations are done on a table:
	I : Insert
	D : Delete 
	U : Update

Assumptions......
1. No operation at Centre till the time backup is taken, imported into Zonal System, Exported from Zonal System and restored at the Centre.
2. While exporting, the database is created afresh, and the data is INSERTed from the Zonal System.
3. For some entities that will be managed by the Centre (like Sewas) will have IDs partitioned for each of the centres (indicated by * below).
